## What is qfs?

qfs exposes every external service through **one uniform, filesystem-shaped, pipe-SQL DSL**. It
runs as a **CLI** locally, as a **daemon** on EC2, or (target) compiled to `wasm32` for Cloudflare
Workers. See the [design anchor (RFD-0001)](https://github.com/qmu/qfs/blob/main/.workaholic/RFDs/0001-qfs-architecture.md)
and the [crate-boundary architecture](https://github.com/qmu/qfs/blob/main/packages/qfs/ARCHITECTURE.md).

These docs are generated by the qfs build tooling and served with VitePress. The reference pages
(Language, Drivers, Server) carry a do-not-edit banner because they are regenerated from the
binary; the [docs index](/README) and the [ADRs](/adr/0001-parser-library) are hand-written.
